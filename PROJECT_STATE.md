# Project State: Oslo Macro and Market-cycle Opportunity Radar

Last updated: 2026-05-26

## Core Aim

Build a private-first research radar that answers one central question:

> Where are global equities, major sectors, and Oslo-linked subsectors in the macro and market cycle now, and what evidence points to continuation, transition, recovery, deterioration, or exit risk?

The project should not become a broad data catalog. New indicators are useful only if they improve cycle-status classification, transition detection, contradiction analysis, or source confidence.

The radar is not an investment-advice engine and does not pick single stocks. It is a structured starting point for further analyst work.

## Target End State

The static report should synthesize public/open indicators into:

- global equity cycle status;
- growth cycle status;
- inflation and rates pressure status;
- liquidity and credit status;
- market-pricing and risk-appetite status;
- sector/subsector phase labels;
- transition or continuation warnings;
- contradicting evidence;
- source health, missing-data caveats, and confidence.

Expected phase language should be explicit and evidence-backed, for example:

- early recovery candidate;
- recovery confirmation;
- mid-cycle continuation;
- late-cycle/crowded risk;
- deterioration/downturn;
- transition watch;
- insufficient evidence.

## Operating Rules

- Do not bypass paywalls, CAPTCHAs, bot blocks, or restricted content.
- Prefer official APIs, official bulk downloads, or stable public CSV endpoints.
- Every new connector must produce source-status rows, freshness metadata, clear failure messages, and tests.
- New data should enter scoring or synthesis only after fallback behavior is visible and strict live builds fail on numeric `sample_fallback`.
- Missing dimensions must be shown as missing, proxied, or sample-backed, not treated as neutral.
- Local/private material must stay out of GitHub and public exports.
- Unreviewed research claims must remain separate from numeric scoring unless explicit confidence rules are implemented.
